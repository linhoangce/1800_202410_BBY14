Who are we?

Founded in 2024, Farmify was developed by Linh Hoang, Joseph Jahanshahi, and Isaac Azage. Inspired by making produce accessible and affordable for the community, the group prototyped Farmify, when the app increased in popularity, Farmify officially launched Feb 05, 2024. The group now lives in Vancouver, BC where they are working to completing their diploma in Computer Systems Technology at the British Columbia Institute of Technology.
